About


I'm a full-stack developer with a background in psychology and the arts. Prior to tech, my experience has bridged fields from picture framing to being a full-time singer and songwriter. As a result, I'm a detail-oriented person with a passion for efficiency and creating things that people can actually use in their everyday lives, whether it's a favorite song, a beautifully framed family photo, or an easy-to-use web app.

I graduated from CodeSquad Boston in 2023 with a certificate in full-stack web development. My technical skills include fluency in JavaScript, React, EJS, Node.js, MongoDB, GoogleOAuth, HTML, CSS, and Git.

When I'm not coding, I'm either playing music or playing video-games. My main instruments are guitar and voice, and I'm always down to jam or sing with friends. I also enjoy working on the hardware side of computers too - I completed my first PC build in 2021.
